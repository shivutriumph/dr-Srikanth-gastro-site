A Legacy of Surgical Excellence

Dr. Srikanth Gadiyaram is a distinguished Senior Surgical Gastroenterologist and founder-director of Sahasra Hospitals. Trained at JIPMER, Pondicherry and SGPGIMS, Lucknow, he completed an advanced fellowship in Laparoscopic & Bariatric Surgery at the University of Minnesota under Prof. Sayed Ikramuddin.
